3. The Gate System

Gates are your primary source of MP. They spawn in waves every 3 whole rounds (meaning after every living player has finished their turn).

  • Difficulty Curve: Early Game (Rank E and D gates), Mid Game (Rank C and B gates), Late Game (Rank A gates).
  • Farming: To defeat a gate, you must land on its tile. If your MP is higher than the gate‚Äôs MP, you absorb its power. If you are weaker, you will fall.

4. Combat & Runes (Power-ups)

When you land on another player or a Gate, a 5-second Battle Phase begins.

Combat Rules

  • The hunter with the higher MP wins.
  • The winner absorbs the loser's MP.
  • In PvP, the loser is eliminated.

The Runes

Runes are randomly obtained by defeating Gates and can only be activated during the 5-second combat window.

Power Runes resolutions

If both players use runes, these are the resolutions.

  • RULER'S POWER VS RULER'S POWER: The attacker always wins (this will also apply when the two players battling has the same MP).
  • RULER'S POWER VS AIR WALK: The battle cancels and you get teleported on random block in the board (the AIR WALK user).

    • Whatever rune used or in any battle, AIR WALK is your greatest escape.

Exhaustion and Cowardly stuns

If you run too much, you will be hit by these stuns.

  • EXHAUSTION STUN: After 5 turns without battling any gates or other players, you get stunned for 1 turn.
  • COWARDLY STUN: After 10 turns without battling any other players, you get stunned by 2 turns.

    • You need to make each of your moves intentional, nothing wasted to avoid being stunned.

      5. The Silver Monarch & Winning

      The ultimate goal is to defeat the Silver Monarch.

      • Spawn Condition: Descends only when one player remains (Total, including AIs).
      • The Trial: You have 4 turns to reach and defeat the Monarch.
      • Power Level: The Monarch‚Äôs MP ranges from 1,500 to 17,000.
      • Victory: Defeating the Monarch ends the game and grants Hunter Points (HuP).
      • The Reawakening: If the last player loses or time hits zero, everyone respawns, gates are cleared, and Rank S and A gates appear everywhere, and all of the fallen players will receive a random 500-1500 MP bonus.
